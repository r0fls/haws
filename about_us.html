Who we are

I believe introductions are in order...

Synergy

Raphael and Lech met in middle school at the Franciscan Montessori Earth School and went to high school together at De La Salle North Catholic. While they won't be preaching the gospel to you at any point, they do possess an unrivaled level of ethics and professionalism that was fostered through years of community-focused educational curriculum.


Raphael Deem

Raphael is an avid open source contributor. His technical skills cover everything web-related from operations to full stack development, as well as non-web-technology such as general automation. Whether you need database optimization, sophisticated caching policies, API development, or something else: he can help.

Equipped with a math degree from Reed College, Raphael enjoys the most challenging technical problems. At Reed, he wrote his thesis on algebraic error correcting codes, which provide accurate signal transmissions in everything from cell phones to televisions. When he's not coding, he's probably sleeping.
